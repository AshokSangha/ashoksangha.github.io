History & Milestones

Founded in 1965 (edit this), Baghbazar Ashok Sangha has promoted cultural programs, social welfare, and youth engagement in North Kolkata.

1965 — Club established by local residents and volunteers.
1978 — First annual sports meet and blood donation camp.
2002 — Introduced scholarship program for students.
2016 — Launched community health check-up drive.
2024 — Digital registration launched for events.
Mission

To foster unity, preserve culture, and empower our community through sports, cultural activities, education, and service.
